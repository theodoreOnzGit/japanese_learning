## Summary

Google's announcement of Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS. Japanese is one of the
model's higher-quality evaluated languages; reviews note it reproduces
natural intonation/emotion without special tuning. Referenced as a possible
listening reference for pitch-accent practice, since natural-sounding
Japanese TTS depends on getting pitch accent right — not a substitute for
feedback on the user's own pronunciation.
