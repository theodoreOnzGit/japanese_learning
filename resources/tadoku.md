**Used in:** `N5/quiz_mode.md` (reading comprehension question type)

## Summary

A Japanese extensive-reading ("tadoku") platform run by an NPO. Offers 150+
free graded readers with furigana, illustrations, and audio narration,
organized by proficiency level, plus community classes for learners and
educators. Good source for level-appropriate reading passages.
